Stir Fry

Ingredients

  1. Green Bellpeppers
  2. Jalapenos
  3. Olives
  4. Tomato Sauce
  5. Soy Sauce
  6. Chicken or Beef

What is Stir Fry?

Stir-Fry is a delicious recipe that encompasses sizeable portions from all food-groups. The dish is mainly comprised of a vegetable base, sprinkled in with any meat of choice. Complimented with a variety of sauces, marinating the tastebuds with a mixture of sweet & salty.
