Chips & Salsa

Ingredients:

  1. Tortilla Chips
  2. Tomato Sauce
  3. Diced Tomatos
  4. Green Peppers
  5. Cilantro

What are Chips & Salsa?

Chips & Salsa are a staple within the hand food category. Great for any occasion, whether it be board game night or your grand mother's funeral. Nothing beats the zest that comes with the refreshness of the tomato fruit; mixed with the finest spices shared from the culture of our hispanic friends. Chips & Salsa is a go-to when it comes to stepping up a notch in the party-game.
